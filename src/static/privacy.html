Privacy

We collect and manage your personal information as described on this page, unless stated otherwise.

Information we collect

We may ask you to provide personal information through this site. We will only do this to provide you this site's service.

We only collect:

  • Student IDs, to be used as the username to log into the site
  • Date of births, to be used as passwords to log into the site
  • Student's year level for restricting students to certain sports

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Addresses
  • Emails
  • Phone numbers

We will not add you to a mailing list, or give your personal information to third parties without your consent, unless required by law.

Cookies

We will only use cookies to identify you once you have logged in. These cookies will be used to provide you with this site's service. No cookies will be used if you have not logged in.

Security

Date of births and other passwords are stored in a salted and hashed format, making it practially impossible to decrypt.

Parts of this site transmit information securely across the internet. However this is not always feasible. We recognise that there may be risks transmitting information across the internet. We will notify you where personal information is not transmitted securely.

Where we process your personal information

Your information will be processed and stored within Australia.
